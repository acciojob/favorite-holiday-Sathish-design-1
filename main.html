Paris


Paris, the City of Light, is known for its romantic charm, stunning architecture, and world-famous landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum.

  1. Eiffel Tower – An iconic symbol offering breathtaking city views.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the Mona Lisa and other masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Seine River Cruise – Offers a scenic and peaceful experience.
  5. Champs-Élysées – Famous for shopping and vibrant atmosphere.
  • Crepes – Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Croissants – Buttery and flaky pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • French Onion Soup – A comforting dish topped with melted cheese.
  • Macarons – Colorful, delicate sweets with various flavors.
  • Baguette – The classic French bread, crispy and fresh.
